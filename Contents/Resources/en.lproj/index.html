iRoleMaster

iRoleMaster is ment as a suplimentary tool for playing RoleMaster®, originally published by Iron Crown Entertainment (ICE). Even though several modules are made available through this program, you should still have these modules in paper for playing.

This is a supporting document to iRoleMaster app, which might deviate from Rolemaster®. Any documentation of ICE products are meant for developers, and not as a supplement of owning your own copy of the core rules.

Below is found lists of ICE products. Rules and optional rules from these publications might and might not have been considered into the finished iRoleMaster app. Some of these rules might have been altered for game balance purposes, and should not be used as direct references to this app.

Note: iRoleMaster combines skills, categories, spell system and more from several generations and settings of Rolemaster®, so cross-referencing is not good for comparison.
